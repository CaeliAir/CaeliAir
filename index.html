Our Mission

At Caéli Air, we strive to capture the best of Australian air, sourced from multiple locations and stored at an average pressure of 1013.25 millibars.

Our products are vessles of air sealed at various locations. They are novelty items, something to put around the house to start conversation and remind you of the importance of sustainability. Or you could breathe it.

Giving Back

With air pollution becoming an ever increasing issue, we pledge to donate 30% of all profits towards the mitigation of air pollution and other environmental issues such as climate change.
